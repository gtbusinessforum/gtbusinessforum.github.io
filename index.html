What is the Georgia Tech Business Forum?

The Georgia Tech Business Forum is back for its 5th year, stronger than ever. This time around, we're really honing in on exposing students to the various facets of the business world. Through interactive workshops, engaging speakers, and dedicated networking, GTBF provides opportunities for students to gain a stronger grasp on business skills.

This year’s theme encourages attendees to consider outside-the-box applications and interesting nuances of typical business skills, including marketing, investing, and entrepreneurship. Being college students, we often find ourselves losing touch with the tangible and interesting applications of our skills. This year's theme seeks to help students find their connection to business and leverage it to springboard their future.
