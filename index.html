What is the Georgia Tech Business Forum?

The Georgia Tech Business Forum is back for its 5th year, stronger than ever. This time around, we're really honing in on exposing students to the various facets of the business world. Through workshops, speakers, and networking, GTBF provides the opportunities for students to gain a stronger grasp on business skills and connect them to their careers.

This year’s theme is Be Aware. Be Ready. Be Bold., encouraging attendees to consider outside-the-box applications and interesting nuances of typical business skills, including marketing, investing, and entrepreneurship. Being college students, we often find ourselves losing touch with the tangible and interesting applications of our skills. This year's theme seeks to help students find their connection to business and leverage it to springboard their future.

Learn. Explore. Innovate.


  • Learn from experienced industry professionals.
  • Network with top Fortune 100 companies and professionals.
  • Connect with aspiring leaders of the Georgia Tech community and Greater Atlanta area.
